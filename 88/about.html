About

A short History

Rush fm is the first private radio station of its kind in Jalingo, Taraba State. Born out of the desire to bring good, quality and undiluted entertainment to her Listeners, the radio station also seeks to ensure quick dissemination of information amongst her many listeners.


A vibe of positivity is blowing around Taraba State and Rush FM is determined to be the Radio FM with a big difference. We will definitely leave our marks on the sand of time
